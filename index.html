Installations and Simulated Environments

The Works shown here start with the latest ones and the oldest one dates back to 2015. They are outcomes of both my collaborative and independent practice as a digital media art practitioner. You will find examples of XR projects, Algorithmic Designs, and Data-Driven Installations.

Reflect

XR Project 2021. In collaboration with Kimberly Davis and AliceLab @YorkU

Somatic Interventions

Bio-Data Driven Web Installation 2021. In collaboration with Ilze Briede, Nick Fox Gieg, Kieran Maraj, and Vertical Studio Lab II under the direction of Mark-David Hosale.

Thumos

Kinetic Algorithmically-Driven Installation 2020-ongoing. Under the Supervision of Mark-David Hosale and Joel Ong.

Ripples

Algorithmically-Driven Light Boxes 2019. In collaboration with DispersionLab, as part of VSL I under the supervision of Doug Van Nort.

ArtWorld

A-Life Theatre 2019. In collaboration with Malcolm Harriot and under the supervision of Graham Wakefield.

Morphogenesis

A-Life Simulation 2018. Under the supervision of Graham Wakefield.

Anemos

Kinetic Environmental Data-Driven Installation. In collaboration with Rory Hoy and under the Supervision of Joel Ong.

Floor Sensors

Interactive Music-Making Installation. Pop-up Exhibition 2018.

You've been Framed

Computer Vision Installation Work 2015. Under the supervision of Andrew Prior @Plymouth University
